Descripcion

The Seven Sisters is the 39th tallest waterfall in Norway. The 410-meter tall waterfall consist of seven separate streams, and the tallest of the seven has a free fall that measures 250 metres. The waterfall is located along the Geirangerfjorden in Stranda Municipality in More og Romsdal conuntry, Norway
